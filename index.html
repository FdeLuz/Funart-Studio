ABOUT US

Funart Studio is an art studio founded by Luz Fernandez and Franco Grassano in hopes to bring the public fun and colorful illustrations that bring the childwonder to the viewer. From Concept art to magazines, their illustration inspired work hopes to bring joy to all that see it!

Luz Fernandez

Luz is an Argentinian artist and character designer based in Buenos aires, Argentina. She loves working with bright colors and experimenting with new ideas for amazing stories.

While she is still learning, working in character creation for animation and videogames is her dream, and she hopes you are willing to follow her journey to achieving it!

Franco Grassano

Franco is an Argentinian artist and designer based in Buenos aires, Argentina. He loves working in creative designs and illustrar various characters from popular movies and t.v series.

While still learning, working designing logos, magazines and even web pages is his dream and he hopes you follow him on his journey!
